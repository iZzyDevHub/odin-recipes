Homemade Pizza

Description

A simple, crispy homemade pizza with fresh mozzarella and basil.

This dough is easy to handle and results in a perfect golden crust every time.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 450Â°F.
  2. Roll out the dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread a thin layer of sauce and add cheese.
  4. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is brown.
  5. Top with fresh basil and a drizzle of olive oil.
