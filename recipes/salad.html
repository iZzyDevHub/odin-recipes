Greek Salad

Description

A refreshing and healthy Greek salad featuring crisp cucumbers and salty feta cheese.

Itâ€™s the perfect side dish for any summer meal or a light lunch on its own.

Ingredients

  • Cucumbers
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Kalamata olives
  • Feta cheese
  • Red onion
  • Greek dressing

Steps

  1. Wash and chop all vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Combine vegetables and olives in a large bowl.
  3. Toss with Greek dressing until well coated.
  4. Crumble feta cheese over the top before serving.
