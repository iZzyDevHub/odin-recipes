Lasagna

Description

A classic, meaty lasagna with layers of pasta, rich tomato sauce, and creamy cheese.

This recipe is perfect for family dinners and leftovers the next day.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Marinara sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles in boiling water.
  2. Brown the beef and mix with sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese in a pan.
  4. Bake at 375Â°F for 45 minutes.
